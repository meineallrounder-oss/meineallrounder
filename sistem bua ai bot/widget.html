This is an example page showing how to integrate the chatbot widget into your website.

Integration Steps:

  1. Copy the chat/ folder to your website
  2. Create .env file with your OpenAI API key
  3. Customize config.php with your company information
  4. Add the widget HTML and CSS/JS to your pages

  • config.php: Customize with your company information
  • chat-admin.php: Change password (line 14)
